Internally QuincyKit uses two additional pieces of software: The PLCrashReporter framework, and the protobuf-c library. QuincyKit and PLCrashReporter have been released under the MIT license, while protobuf-c is available under the Apache License v2.0. This version of %applicationName% uses QuincyKit HEAD, which is currently at commit 443214387fccae5558e9a660474ec8482f0a8c9d.
